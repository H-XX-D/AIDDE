# Concept

AIDDE stands for Artificial Intelligence Driven Development Environment.

The name matters because the product is not just another IDE with AI attached.
It describes the category: a development environment where AI agents are
first-class workers, and the interface is built around observing, routing, and
controlling them.

## The core bet

AI coding is not blocked by model capability alone. It is blocked by the
interface.

Users need to know:

- what context is about to be used,
- what memory was actually used,
- what files were touched,
- what commands ran,
- what tests passed or failed,
- what risk changed,
- what can be stopped before it becomes expensive or dangerous.

AIDDE makes those things visible.

## Product shape

AIDDE is planned as a desktop app with a bounded internal coordinate system:

- panels are typed surfaces,
- sidebars and center panels use the same primitive,
- every panel can carry permissions and routing,
- agents can be aimed at panels,
- command input lives at the bottom of the app frame,
- memory preflight is visible where the user is already looking,
- the audit panel records action, not prose.

## Why DD exists

DD, also known as DeeDee, gives the runtime a face.

The face is not a gimmick. It gives state a natural place to live:

- awake or asleep,
- listening or quiet,
- active or idle,
- pinned or sidecar-hidden,
- normal window behavior or companion behavior.

The product should feel approachable without becoming vague. DD is the soft
surface over a serious operational system.
